Nearby and View - Simple

Some things just take more than a week to develop, so I am just going with the idea here. Amongst many things e-commerce has failed to do is earn the trust of the common middle class man, it is very evident they cannot replace Sharma Ji's shop. By introducing a masked middle-man, it is very hard to trust e-commerce websites, atleast not in the full potential.


One of the things that can be done is to introduce a location based e-commerce network. This cuts out the middle-man, ensures job security of most local shops and definetly earns the trust of the consumers as they would be buying from shops they know.




Another impressive add-on would be to use Microsoft's Image Recognition Cognitive services to make these services interactive. So basically with a webcam, mobile cameras you can make the platform recognize the products you want and search for it using Location based e-comm search. We've always seen stuff in magazines, now we can just click those pictures and get your items right on.




This idea also promises to create jobs, especially for local shops and eco-friendly bicycle delivery businesses.




The need for a location-based e-commerce is very obvious, for a better automated lifestyle of course!
